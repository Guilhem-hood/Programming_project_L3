Why build it

The concept

What if your personal archive behaved like a tactile map? Memories Atlas fuses 3D cartography, storytelling, and sensory UI to form an emotional atlas you can orbit.

01. Import & geotag

Keep EXIF GPS data or drop pins manually, then add context such as companions, weather, sounds, and feelings.

02. Sculpt spatial stories

Curate collections, sequence camera paths, and blend music or voiceover so every tour feels cinematic.

03. Share immersive capsules

Publish private capsules, collaborative trips, or AI-assisted travel logs that friends can experience on any device.

About the builder

Guilhem — explorer, cyclist, geomatics nerd

I chase remote landscapes through mountain sports and long-distance rides, always hunting for new perspectives that only a summit or a winding trail can unlock. That curiosity naturally converged with my love for geomatics and cartography.

Memories Atlas is my way to merge field exploration and spatial storytelling: a place where every photo becomes a waypoint and every journey can be replayed on a living map.
